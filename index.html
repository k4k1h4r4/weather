⚙ Spray application limits
Adjust these sliders to match your chemical label's application requirements. Spray Window — Right Now
Checks four conditions against your current limit settings and gives a single go/no-go answer. Wind: below your min risks temperature inversions that trap and drift chemical; above your max causes physical off-target drift. Humidity: too low and droplets evaporate before reaching the leaf; too high and product won't dry or may run off. Temperature: below your min slows plant absorption; above your max causes volatile herbicides (especially auxins like dicamba/2,4-D) to turn to vapor and drift. Precipitation: any active rain washes product off before it can work.
